About Starkson Packaging Inc.

Food Safety and Quality Policy

At Starkson Packaging Inc., we are committed to providing safe, high-quality, and reliable food-grade packaging materials, promotional items, and sourcing programs. Our goal is to consistently meet customer expectations, achieve business success, and ensure that all products are safe and comply with all relevant standards, statutory and regulatory requirements.

To achieve this, SPI shall:

  • Implement and maintain a robust Food Safety and Quality Management System (FSQMS) that promotes safe practices, accountability, and continuous improvement.
  • Ensure all products consistently meet established specifications and procedures.
  • Provide innovative packaging solutions and promotional programs while maintaining safety and quality.
  • Deliver products and services on time to meet customer needs.
  • Train and empower all employees to understand and follow SPI’s food safety requirements, emphasizing hygiene, safe practices, and responsibility for the quality of their work.
  • Conduct hazard analysis and implement appropriate control measures, including HACCP, to prevent risks to food safety.
  • Review, audit, and continuously improve systems, processes, and performance at all levels of the business.

Senior Management ensures adequate organization, resources, equipment, and training to achieve SPI’s food safety and quality objectives. All employees share responsibility for maintaining product safety and quality and are required to cooperate fully to meet statutory and regulatory obligations.

This policy is communicated to all employees and relevant stakeholders and is reviewed regularly to ensure it remains relevant and effective.
